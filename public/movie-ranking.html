Top 3 Action Packed Movies by Jojo

My Top 3 go-to action movies are:

Extraction I & II

I love the die-hard spirit of the main character, Tyler Rake, played by Chris Hemsworth. The intense action sequences and gripping storyline make it a must-watch for action movie fans.

Mission Impossible I, II, III, & IV

The Mission Impossible series, starring Tom Cruise as Ethan Hunt, is a classic in the action genre. The high-stakes missions, incredible stunts, and thrilling plot twists keep me on the edge of my seat every time.

Fast and Furious I, II, III, IV, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, & X

The Fast and Furious franchise is a rollercoaster of adrenaline-pumping action, fast cars, and epic heists. The camaraderie among the characters and the over-the-top action scenes make it an absolute blast to watch.
